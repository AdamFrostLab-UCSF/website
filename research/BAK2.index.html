The Frost integrates genetic, biochemical, and imaging approaches to discover molecular machines and to learn how they function within the cell. We use genetics to identify multi-component complexes and to annotate their functions, and we leverage advances in cryo-EM and image analysis to resolve the structures of these machines in their functional states.
